FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

1.  What do I need to sign-up to the service?

 You need one of the following methods in order to sign up:

  1. a) Email account
  2. b) Mobile number
  3. c) Facebook account
  4. d) Google account

2.  What do I do if I cannot sign into my account?

Firstly, please ensure that you are logging-in using the correct login method (that is the option that you selected to sign-up to the service – refer point 1)

  1. a) Login using email address:
    • If you are using the correct log-in method, double check that you typed in the correct email address

     

  2. b) Forgot password:
    • If you forgot your login password, then enter your username and use the forgot password option to reset your password
    • A password reset link will be sent to your email address
    • Open the link
    • Create a new password
    • Login to the service

     

  3. c) Login using your mobile number:
    • If mobile number was your method to sign up and you cannot login, check if you typed-in the correct number
    • If the number is correct, you will receive an OTP
    • Enter the OTP on the verify screen
    • If the OTP fails to verify, double check the OTP used or request a new one

     

  4. d) Not receiving an OTP:
    • If you did not receive an OTP, double check the mobile number typed in (it should be the one you registered your account with)
      • If the mobile number used is correct and If your network connection is good, you should receive the OTP SMS within 60 seconds after you clicked ’continue’/ ‘submit’
      • If you have not received the OTP in 60 seconds, use the resend OTP option and check your mobile phone for an SMS
    • Your mobile number must be active to receive an OTP
    • Your mobile number must be connected to your mobile network and with sufficient signal connection to receive an OTP

     

  5. e) Login using Google or Facebook account
    • If you signed up with a Google or Facebook account, you may need to re-authorize your Avvatta login on your Google or Facebook account settings
    • Your Google or Facebook details will be used to verify you as the account holder

3.  How do I subscribe on Avvatta?

Subscribing to Avvatta gives you unlimited access to the content vertical subscribed to. You will have access to the full service for the period you have subscribed (daily, weekly or monthly)  

  • Subscription can be done in 2 ways:
    • Launch Avvatta (open in your browser)
    • Sign-in or Sign-up
    • If subscribing from the Home Screen , select the burger menu button at the top menu bar of the screen (by clicking on your profile name)
    • Select the My Account tab
    • Enter your 4-digit PIN/Login password when prompted
    • Select the Subscriptions Management tab under My Account
    • Browse to the subscription content vertical that you wish to subscribe to
    • Select the ‘Subscribe’ tab on the subscription option of choice
    • Make payment for your subscription
    • Your subscription will be active for its validity period
    • Enjoy watching

      • OR,

    • Launch Avvatta (open in your browser)
    • Sign-in or Sign-up on Avvatta
    • Select any premium content
    • The subscription options will be displayed for you to choose
    • Select the subscription package of your choice
    • Make payment for your subscription
    • Your subscription will be active for its validity period
    • Enjoy watching

 

4.  How do I cancel my subscription?

    • Once logged in, click on the profile burger menu button on the right of the top menu bar
    • Select the My Account tab
    • Enter your 4-digit PIN/Login password when prompted
    • Click on the ‘Cancel Subscription’ tab
    • Select the active package you wish to cancel
    • Confirm to unsubscribe from the service
    • Your subscription will be cancelled (recurring billing will be discontinued)
    • NOTE – you will have access to the content until the package you cancelled expires

5.  Profile PIN

The ‘Profile PIN’ feature allows the main account holder to create a profile pin – this will allow the account holder to restrict access to account tools and profile switching thus ensuring that age appropriate content is viewed correctly and account information is protected

 

How do I create the Parental passcode?

  • Launch Avvatta (open in browser)
  • On the Home Screen , select the profile burger menu button at the right side of the top menu bar
  • Select the Parental control menu tab
  • Create the 4-digit pin by entering 4-digits of your choice
  • Confirm the 4-digit pin by retyping it
  • Select to save the profile PIN

 

What do I do if I forgot my Profile PIN?

  • Select the ‘Forgot PIN’ option on the Profile PIN pop-up screen
  • Input your registered email address or mobile number (depends on your chosen option at sign up)
  • A link will be sent to your registered email or mobile number with instructions to reset your Profile PIN

 

6.  What does it mean when the subscription packages display when I want to stream?

If the subscription options pop up when content is clicked on it could mean one of the following:

 

  • The content is premium content (paid content) and requires you to subscribe to it
  • Your trial period has expired
  • You have cancelled your subscription and the active package you had has now expired
  • Your subscription renewal was not successful implying that your subscription was not renewed

7.  What tools does the account holder (main profile) have on Avvatta?

The main profile settings offer the following options:

  • a) Create up to four profiles
  • b) Manage profiles
    • Set age limits
    • Edit or Remove profiles
    • Switch profiles
  • c) Manage your account
    • Cancel membership
    • Change billing date and/ or details of billing option
    • View streaming history
